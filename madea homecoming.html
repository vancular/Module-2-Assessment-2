Madea homecoming

It is the twelfth film in the Madea cinematic universe which tells the story of Madea partaking in her great-grandson's college graduation party as hidden secrets emerge. It was released on February 25, 2022. It is adapted from Perry's stage play Madea's Farewell Play, the first Madea film to be adapted from a stage play since A Madea Christmas. The film is also a crossover between the Madea franchise and the Irish sitcom Mrs. Brown's Boys.
